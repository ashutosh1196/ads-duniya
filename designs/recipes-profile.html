Grilled Salmon

40 MIN 236 KCAL
CATEGORIES
8 servings
  • 900g salmon filet with skin
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon
  • 3 tbsp chopped dill
  • 3 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 3 tbsp chopped thyme
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp chili flakes

Grilled salmon is an easy, delicious and healthy option to enjoy for lunch or dinner as the summer rolls on.In just a few simple steps, you can enjoy a meal that's full of the vitamins and minerals you need to feel good day to day.

INSTRUCTIONS
  • preheat the oven to 350'F/175'c.
  • In a bowl add the minced garlic,fresh herbs,zest from 1 lemon,salt,and chili flakes and mix.
  • Place the salmon on parchment paper,and add the herb mixture on top.
  • Bake the salmon in the oven for 20 minutes until it has an inner temp around 125-131'F/52-55'C.
  • Slice the lemon and serve on stop.
